Our sponsors

In a groundbreaking partnership, Coca-Cola began sponsoring our drug awareness platform in 2011, marking a significant commitment to promoting a healthier and informed lifestyle. This collaboration aligns with Coca-Cola's dedication to social responsibility and community engagement. Together, we aim to amplify the impact of our drug education initiatives and contribute to a safer and more informed society.


In a harmonious collaboration that began in 2008, Spotify proudly sponsors our drug awareness platform. Committed to fostering positive conversations, Spotify's partnership enhances our outreach efforts, using the power of music to convey messages of awareness and resilience. Together, we aim to strike a chord with audiences worldwide, promoting a healthier and informed perspective on drug-related issues.


Since 2017, Red Bull has been a dynamic force behind our drug awareness platform, injecting vitality into our mission. This strategic partnership reflects Red Bull's commitment to supporting initiatives that promote well-being and knowledge. Together, we aim to energize discussions around drug education, empowering individuals to make informed choices for a healthier and more vibrant future.
